The San Francisco Federal Building Plaza was a redesign project that started out as a submission to a competition hosted by the government building. The prompt for the competition was open ended: “Envision a design intervention that activates the New San Francisco Federal Building plaza.” In a team of four, I worked as the architect and visual planner. Synthesizing city planning theories, empirical observations and interviews of plaza visitors, and the research my group came up with, I re-designed the look and feel of the plaza.
